Veggie Omelette

Description

This omelette is a healthy source of protein, it contains veggies and is a very flexible recipe.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic Powder
  • Any additional vegetables

Instructions

  1. First dice vegetables until they are less than a centimeter in diameter.
  2. Crack 3 eggs and put them into a hot pan and stir the eggs until color is uniform.
  3. Pour diced vegetables into the pan with the eggs then wait for it to cook.
  4. When bottom side of egg is cooked flip it halfway up and continue to do this until the egg is all rolled up.
